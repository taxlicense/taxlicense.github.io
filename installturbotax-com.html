How to Purchase Turbotax?

  1. Go to the official TurboTax website.
  2. Choose the TurboTax product that best suits your tax situation.
  3. Once you select the product, click the “Buy Now” or “Get Started for Free” button.
  4. If you have an existing TurboTax account, log in. If not, you will need to create a new account.
  5. After logging in, add the selected product to your cart.
  6. Review your order in the shopping cart. Click "Checkout" or similar button to proceed.
  7. Provide your payment details, such as credit card information.
  8. Double check your order details and payment information.
  9. Click "Submit" or similar button to complete your purchase.

How to Create TurboTax Account?

  1. Visit installturbotax.com website.
  2. Choose the correct TurboTax version.
  3. Click "Create Account".
  4. Enter personal details and create a password.
  5. Set security questions.
  6. Agree to the terms and conditions.
  7. Verify your email by clicking the link sent by TurboTax.
  8. Select the appropriate tax product.
  9. Your account has been created, now you can login.

Turbotax license code

The license code is a combination of letters and numbers (XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX) that verifies the software and allows you to finish installing and begin using TurboTax.

Where to Find It?


Here are two ways to find the license code number:

a) CD Box:

It can be found on CD box envelopes, inserts, or sealing slips. Check the CD box for the license code.


B) Confirmation Email:

The code will appear in the confirmation email or order history after you install the software


Download and Install Turbotax : Installturbotax.com

  1. Sign in to your TurboTax account at turbotax.ca/download.
  2. Select the "Downloads" tab.
  3. Click on the blue button for the product you want to download.

What is TurboTax Live Full Service?

With TurboTax Live Full Service, a tax specialist does your taxes for you from start to finish. You will be introduced to a tax expert who will ensure you get every dollar you are entitled to, guaranteed. You can connect with your expert in real time and receive your taxes without any wait and surprises.


Can you reset the TurboTax login e-file?

Yes, you can change your filing option to e-file. But make sure you haven’t already sent the tax return mail. If you use TurboTax Online and choose that you need to print and mail the tax return, then TurboTax login will treat the return as filed. If that happens, you will not see the “Take me to my return/ Continue my return" section on your TurboTax login account.
